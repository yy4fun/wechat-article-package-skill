# Prompt Template

Use this template when the user asks for a polished Chinese WeChat cover and does not provide a stronger visual direction.

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: Chinese WeChat official account cover image, exact 2.35:1 wide landscape composition
Primary request: Create an eye-catching cover image for a Chinese article about <ARTICLE TOPIC>. The reader hook is <HOOK>.

Visual style: flat hand-drawn doodle illustration, fresh soft Macaron Colors, cute but polished editorial style. Use peach pink, mint green, cream yellow, pale blue, and warm off-white. Background should be a soft watercolor wash or gentle pastel gradient, airy and light.

Scene/backdrop: a clean pastel workspace with rounded irregular pebble/cloud shapes. Add simple black-line doodle elements that match the article topic: <2-4 relevant objects>. Add 1-2 small icon doodles such as <rocket/lightning/shield/chart/calculator/document>, plus stars, dashed lines, and small circles.

Subject: <one-sentence visual metaphor for the article>. Keep it friendly, lightweight, and easy to understand.

Composition/framing: 2.35:1 wide cover. Main visual elements centered or slightly left. Right side reserved for large Chinese title text on a soft rounded blob. Keep lots of whitespace and avoid clutter.

Text (verbatim): Large title: "<TITLE, 8 Chinese characters or fewer>". Small subtitle below: "<SHORT SUBTITLE>". Use modern rounded sans-serif Chinese typography, dark gray or black, crisp and readable.

Lighting/mood: bright, gentle, young, healing, smart, low-pressure.

Constraints: flat fill colors with simple black outlines; no photorealism, no thick 3D, no realistic people, no logos, no brand marks, no watermark, no English title unless the user asks, no random letters, no distorted text. Make the title clearly readable.
```

## Example From Finance-AI Article

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: Chinese WeChat official account cover image, exact 2.35:1 wide landscape composition
Primary request: Create an eye-catching cover image for a Chinese article about how Anthropic's finance team uses Claude to explain the business story behind changing numbers. The reader hook is: AI is not just writing reports; it helps finance teams understand why numbers change.

Visual style: flat hand-drawn doodle illustration, fresh soft Macaron Colors, cute but polished business editorial style. Use peach pink, mint green, cream yellow, pale blue, and warm off-white. Background should be a soft watercolor wash or gentle pastel gradient, airy and light.

Scene/backdrop: a clean pastel workspace with rounded irregular pebble/cloud shapes. On the left-center, draw a simple finance dashboard sheet with charts, coins, calculator, and connected dots suggesting AI context. Add a tiny rocket and a lightning bolt, plus stars, dashed lines, and small circles.

Subject: a finance team using AI to understand why numbers change; charts, reporting, CFO workflow, business explanation. No realistic office and no heavy corporate tone.

Composition/framing: 2.35:1 wide cover. Main visual elements centered or slightly left. Right side reserved for large Chinese title text on a soft cream-yellow rounded blob. Keep lots of whitespace and avoid clutter.

Text (verbatim): Large title: "数字会说话". Small subtitle below: "Claude 帮财务解释变化". Use modern rounded sans-serif Chinese typography, dark gray or black, crisp and readable.

Lighting/mood: bright, gentle, young, healing, smart, low-pressure.

Constraints: flat fill colors with simple black outlines; no photorealism, no thick 3D, no realistic people, no logos, no brand marks, no watermark, no English title except the word Claude in subtitle, no random letters, no distorted text. Title must be 8 Chinese characters or fewer and clearly readable.
```
